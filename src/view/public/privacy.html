Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

Settled is a debt tracking and payoff planning web application ("we", "us", "our"). Our website is at settlednow.co.uk.

For the purposes of UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, Settled is the data controller.

Contact us at: hello@settlednow.co.uk

2. What data we collect

Account data
When you create an account we collect your email address and a hashed password. We never store your password in plain text.

Debt data
The financial information you enter into Settled — including debt names, balances, interest rates, payment amounts, and payoff goals. This data is entered voluntarily by you and used solely to provide the service.

Payment data
If you upgrade to Settled Plus we collect confirmation that a payment has been made via Stripe. We do not store your card details — these are handled entirely by Stripe.

Technical data
Basic technical information such as browser type and IP address, collected automatically by our infrastructure providers.

3. How we use your data

  • Provide and maintain the Settled service
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Sync your debt data across devices
  • Process payments and verify premium access
  • Send transactional emails such as account confirmation and password reset
  • Respond to support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the service.

4. Legal basis for processing

  • Contract performance — processing your account and debt data is necessary to provide the service
  • Legitimate interests — maintaining security, preventing fraud, and improving the service
  • Legal obligation — retaining payment records as required by HMRC

6. Data retention

We retain your account and debt data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by HMRC financial record-keeping obligations.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, port, object to, and restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise any right, email hello@settlednow.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

8. Cookies

Settled uses a single session cookie to keep you signed in. This is strictly necessary and does not require consent. We use no advertising or analytics cookies.

9. Security

Your data is protected by 256-bit TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage, row-level security ensuring users can only access their own data, and hashed password storage.

10. Children

Settled is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

11. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time and will notify you of significant changes by email.
